# KYOUKAI evolution rules

KYOUKAI may change, but the reason for change must remain visible.

## Required rules

- KYOUKAIの変化は記録する
- 変更理由を残す
- 何を見て変えたかを残す
- 結果が悪ければ戻せるようにする
- 自己増殖は暴走ではなく、記録された改善循環である

## Human control

Evolution records do not grant implementation permission.
They explain why a change happened or why a change is being considered.

## Rollback mindset

Each evolution entry should preserve enough context to understand what changed and what should be watched next.
