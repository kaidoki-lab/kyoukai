# KYOUKAI feedback loop

PHASE 7 defines a recorded improvement loop.
It is not uncontrolled self-editing.

## Loop

観測
↓
解釈
↓
提案
↓
生成物
↓
採用判断
↓
反映
↓
再観測

## Meaning

### 観測

KYOUKAI内で何が起きたかを記録する。
Current PHASE 6 observations are manual and sample-based.

### 解釈

Observation is read as a possible signal.
It is not treated as a command.

### 提案

Supervisor proposals may describe what could change.
`codexReady=false` remains implementation blocked.

### 生成物

Generated candidates can support a proposal.
They are not automatically adopted.

### 採用判断

Human approval is required before any change is made.

### 反映

Only approved changes are implemented.
Routes, pages, and revenue paths are not created from cycle records alone.

### 再観測

After a manual change, the result is observed again and recorded.
